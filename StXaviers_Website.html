Our Story

Founded in 2017 with a bold vision of delivering world-class education in Cuttack, St. Xavier's High School, Jagatpur has been shaping young minds with distinction. We blend rich traditional values with modern, innovative teaching methodologies to create a vibrant learning atmosphere where every student discovers their true potential and is prepared to face tomorrow's world with confidence.

"Education is not just about acquiring knowledge, but also about developing wisdom and integrity. At St. Xavier's, we strive to create an environment where students feel inspired, valued, and prepared for the challenges of tomorrow."
— Principal, St. Xavier's High School, Jagatpur

Admission Process

Enrolling at St. Xavier's is a simple, transparent process. We warmly welcome students from Nursery to Class VIII into our Xavier's family.

  1. 1

    Online Application

    Fill out the online admission form below with complete details and attach all required documents for review.

  2. 2

    Document Verification

    The school's admission team reviews your submitted documents and contacts you for any clarifications needed.

  3. 3

    Interaction Session

    A brief, friendly interaction with the student and parents to understand the child's background and learning needs.

  4. 4

    Fee Payment

    On confirmation, complete the admission by paying the requisite fee at the school office or via bank transfer.

  5. 5

    Welcome to Xavier's! 🎉

    Collect your admission kit, uniform details, and timetable. Your child is now proudly part of the Xavier's family!
